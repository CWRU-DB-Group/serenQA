Instructions for Expertise Evaluators

How the Questions are Organized

The questions are divided into 5 batches based on drug categories. You can refer to batch.json to see the categories for each batch. Each question’s category is labeled as [Category - Category:Subcategory] following the text question.

How the LLM Ranking are Generated

The LLM ranking is generated by evaluating the serendipity of each answer within a question using GPT-4O, GPT-4O-mini, and Claude-3.5-Sonnet, following a specific prompt. Rankings are based on the mean score across these models. The first in line is the most serendipity answer according to the models.

Your Role

Please complete your evaluation following these steps:
1. Review the Question and Rankings:
    · Select at least 2 batches that align with your interest or expertise to evaluate.
    · Carefully review the LLM rankings for each question in your selected batch.
    · Assess whether you agree with the provided order.
2. Provide Your Input:
    · If you agree with the ranking, select Yes.
    · If you disagree, select No and provide your own ranking based on your expertise.
3. Format for Your Ranking:
    · Use the numbers corresponding to the answers in the LLM ranking.
    · Separate the numbers with commas (e.g., 2, 1, 3 for Answer (2) > Answer (1) > Answer (3)).
    · If you are not confident in ranking all answers, you may rank only a subset.
4. Confidence Rating:
    · For each batch, rate your confidence (1–5) in the responses, where 1 indicates the lowest confidence and 5 the highest.

Important Notes

1. To make sure your responses are recorded, please log in before beginning. Your login status is displayed in the side bar.
2. If you refresh or reload the page, or return to evaluate more batches, please log in again using the same email.
3. Batches submitted successfully will be marked with a "✓".
4. If you submit a batch multiple times, only the last submission will be recorded.
5. You may task Batch 6 as a practice batch to familiarize yourself with the evaluation process.
